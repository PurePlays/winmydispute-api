Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2025

1. Introduction

WinMyDispute GPT (“we,” “our,” or “us”) provides an AI-powered chargeback dispute service via our website and API. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Email address: Collected when you initiate a checkout session or create a letter.
  • Payment data: Processed by our payment provider (Stripe). We never store your full card details on our servers.
  • Usage data: Logs, IP addresses, and session IDs to maintain and secure the service.
  • Generated tokens: One-time access tokens for letter generation (stored for up to 7 days).

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To process payments and grant access to the dispute-letter generation service.
  • To generate and deliver dispute letters and related documents.
  • To monitor and improve service quality and performance.
  • To comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our terms.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We share your data only with:

  • Stripe: for payment processing (PCI-compliant).
  • Cloud hosting providers: for API and static file serving.
  • Law enforcement or legal authorities, if required by law.

5. Data Retention

We retain access tokens for up to 7 days or until they are used, and store payment logs for up to 90 days to comply with financial regulations.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To request changes or deletion, contact us at support@winthisdispute.com.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures (encryption, secure servers) to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. When we do, we’ll post the new version with an updated “Last updated” date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please email us at support@winthisdispute.com.
